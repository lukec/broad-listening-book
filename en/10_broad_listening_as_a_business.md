# Chapter 10 Broad Listening as a Business

Written by: tokoroten


As we have seen in the previous chapters, broad listening has begun to be used in a wide range of settings, including elections, local governments, companies, and nonprofits. So who is actually bringing this technology into the field?

It began with volunteers. 広聴AI (Kouchou AI - meaning Public Relations AI), the core tool of broad listening, is developed and released by Digital Democracy 2030 (DD2030), a nonprofit open-source project. However, DD2030 is ultimately a volunteer organization. While it can develop and improve software and provide technical advice, it cannot embed itself in individual on-the-ground settings and provide ongoing operational support.

To fill this gap, businesses emerged around broad listening. Some companies offer implementation consulting for Kouchou AI and help modernize the digital side of election campaigns, while others have launched their own products in adjacent areas such as deliberation and consensus-building, which sit alongside the act of listening.

In this chapter, we introduce the work of DD2030 and the diverse business activities that have grown up around broad listening.
