# Words Audrey Tang Sent for This Book

Broad Listening integrates AI into the cycle of human deliberation,

giving democracy—long biased toward broadcasting—the ability to listen at scale.

Japan is showing the practical answer.


## Supplement

Original text:

> “Broad Listening puts AI in the human loop:
>
>  Democracy learned to broadcast — Japan shows it how to receive.”

Believing that the English version alone would be difficult for Japanese readers to fully grasp, Nishio supplemented the wording in a Japanese translation. Audrey Tang reviewed and approved it together with its English rendering.

English rendering of Nishio’s Japanese translation:

> (Broad Listening brings AI into the loop of human deliberation,
>
> giving democracy—long biased toward broadcasting—the capacity to listen at scale.
>
> Japan is demonstrating a practical answer to this challenge.)
